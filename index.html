Iceland is an island in the Atlantic Ocean, located
near the Arctic Circle, between Greenland and
Norway, An island of 103.000 Kms (40,000 square
miles), it is about the same size as Hungary and
Portugal, or Kentucky and Virginia, Iceland is the
second largest island in Europe, following Great
Britain, and the 18th largest island in the world. The
coastline is 4,970 km, and Iceland maintains a 200
nautical-miles exclusive economic zone.
